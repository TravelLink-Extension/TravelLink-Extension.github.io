TravelLink Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2025

Overview

TravelLink is a Chrome extension that enhances web browsing by making city names clickable and linking them to flight searches. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection

TravelLink collects and stores the following data:

  • Your home airport code (stored locally in your browser)

Data Usage

We use your data only for the following purposes:

  • To provide flight search functionality when you click on city names
  • To remember your preferred home airport for future searches

Data Storage

All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. We do not:

  • Store any data on our servers
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Use your data for any purpose other than the extension's functionality

Permissions Used

  • storage: Used to store your home airport preference locally
  • activeTab: Used to detect and process city names on the current webpage
  • Host permissions: Used to detect city names on webpages you visit

Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete your stored data by uninstalling the extension
  • Change your home airport at any time through the extension's settings
  • Disable the extension at any time

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at TravelLink.Extension@Gmail.com.
